Hello, I'm Tracie Thornbury,
a passionate Technical Artist and Game Designer with a love for crafting immersive gaming experiences.
From my early days, tinkering with Minecraft servers to honing my artistic skills, I've embarked on an exciting journey to bring captivating worlds to life through the art of game design.

My Creative Journey So Far

I am an artist and game enthusiast, passionate about storytelling through interactive experiences. My journey in game design began early, immersed in card games and Pokémon, fueling my love for crafting virtual worlds. As a freelance illustrator, I've honed my skills, breathing life into narratives with captivating visuals. Coaching on Metafy has been both exhilarating and enlightening, mentoring aspiring gamers and sharpening my grasp of competitive gameplay. Beyond gaming, I find joy in supporting my local food bank, driven by the values of unity and collaboration in shaping a better world.

Technical Art and Visual Effects (VFX)

In my independent games "Dracomaton" and "Acquiesce," I showcased my expertise in technical art and visual effects (VFX). For "Dracomaton," I contributed to visually stunning VFX that heightened the game's atmosphere and added depth to in-game enemies and attacks. My role as a concept artist in "Acquiesce" involved crafting captivating character designs.

Playtesting and Balancing

Mastering the intricacies of gaming, I've achieved high rank in half a dozen games. This success reflects my deep understanding of gaming mechanics and strategies, enabling me to craft engaging and immersive gaming experiences. My knowledge of game design principles and player psychology further enhances the quality of my creations.

Collaboration and Communication

As a gaming coach at Metafy, I mentor students, and breaking down complex gaming concepts. This experience has honed my effective verbal communication skills, enabling me to collaborate effortlessly with cross-functional teams in the game development space.

Here's a couple of my projects.

Dracomaton

A roguelike bullet hell featuring a shapeshifting mechanical dragon. Developed during a 20-week jam, I played a pivotal role as the VFX artist, creating stunning visual effects that intensified the gameplay experience. As a dedicated playtester and balancer, I ensured the game achieved optimal challenge and balance, adding depth to the mechanics.

Steam Link.

Acquiesce

In this 2.5D game, you team up with a traditional JRPG party, except your teammates constantly make the worst decisions. As the lead concept artist, I was responsible for crafting captivating character designs, immersive backgrounds, and intuitive UI elements that enriched the gameplay narrative.
Itch Link.

Death Diviner

Prepare for a thrilling bullet hell adventure, where you forge your fate by choosing the obstacles to overcome. In this project, I demonstrated my versatility as an illustrator and UI designer, rapidly creating 18 dynamic card illustrations within a week, adding flair and depth to the game's mechanics.
Itch Link.

Cyber Jump

In this weeklong jam, players conquer a boss in both 2D and 3D, swapping perspectives for an innovative challenge. As the sole artist for the 2D segments, I infused the game with captivating visuals, enhancing the player's immersion throughout the thrilling boss battles.
Itch Link.
